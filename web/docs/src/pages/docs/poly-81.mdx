## Cheap as Chips

Poly 81 is inspired by an affordable polyphonic analog synthesizer released in 1982.

Many compromises were made to hit the low price point, but these limitations gave rise to a delightfully quirky design.

In a world of instant gratification and abundance, why not take inspiration from the creative constraints of a leaner time?

## Vibes-based modeling

While not a faithful replica, Poly 81's design preserves the character and sense of fun from the original synthesizer. It's a love letter to the awkward specialness of that era of transition from analog to digital synthesizers.
