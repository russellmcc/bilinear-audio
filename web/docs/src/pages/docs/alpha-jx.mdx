Built around two DCOs with PWM, Sync, and two envelopes, Alpha JX's deep feature set gives you space to explore the best that late 80s analog technology could have provided. Recreate familiar tones or take it into stranger territory.

</FeatureRow>

<FeatureRow
  heading="What The...!"
  imageSrc={rave.src}
  imageAlt="Warehouse rave"
  reversed
>

With rarely seen features like sawtooth PWM and a novel bit-mod mode,
Alpha JX is adept at the wild "hoover" rave lead sounds of the late 80s
and early 90s. Alpha JX is also at home handling
classic analog basses, plucks, and pads. Modern features like MPE support
provide a level of expressivity that the 1980s never quite achieved.

</FeatureRow>

<ProductSection compact>
## Preservation through Imagination

Drawing on meticulous research into the engineering philosophies of a certain manufacturer who produced their last analog instrument in 1986, Alpha JX imagines a mid-range synth that could have plausibly debuted in early 1987.

</ProductSection>
